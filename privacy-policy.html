Privacy Policy

App: MDR/IVDR Assistant (com.orest.regulation)

Last updated: 2025

1. Data Collection

The app stores data locally on your device: profile settings, niches, saved events. Data is not shared with third parties without your consent.

2. API and External Services

For content generation (event calendar, search, insights) the app uses external AI APIs. Requests contain only technical parameters (niches, country) for response personalisation. Content is not stored by third-party services long-term.

3. Data We Do Not Collect

The app does not collect personal data, email, phone numbers, or location. We do not conduct user analytics.

4. Security

API keys are stored locally on the device. We recommend not sharing them.

5. Contact

Questions about this policy: contact the developer via the app page on Play Store.

6. Changes

We may update this policy. Significant changes will be reflected in app updates.
